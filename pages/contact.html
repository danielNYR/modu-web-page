Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple websites typically take 4-6 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timeline estimates during our initial consultation.

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development methodology with regular client check-ins. Our process includes discovery, planning, design, development, testing, and deployment phases with continuous feedback loops.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. This includes security updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Java, Spring Boot, AWS, Azure, and many others. We choose the best technology stack for each project's specific requirements.
